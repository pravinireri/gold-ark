Our Story

A Tradition of Excellence

Founded in 2022, Gold Ark Restaurant has established itself as a premier destination for those seeking an exceptional dining experience in Thika. Our commitment to culinary excellence and impeccable service continues to earn us recognition throughout the region.

Under the guidance of our culinary team, we have crafted a menu that celebrates both traditional techniques and innovative approaches. We source the finest ingredients, working closely with local farmers and artisans to ensure quality and sustainability.
